Who are Forno?

Forno was born from a simple belief: great food should feel like coming home. Our kitchen celebrates the heart of Italian cooking — honest ingredients, slow craft, and the warmth of sharing a meal with the people who matter. Every dish begins with traditional techniques and the freshest seasonal produce, brought together with a modern touch that reflects our passion for creativity. Whether you join us for a quiet evening or a special celebration, Forno is a place to slow down, savour the moment, and experience Italian hospitality at its finest.
